# Market Analysis Report Types

Reference guide for all available report types and their research angle configurations.

## Overview Report (`overview`)

**Purpose:** Comprehensive daily market overview across all asset classes

**Research Angles (4 parallel):**

1. **Stock Market**
   - S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow Jones performance
   - Sector winners and losers
   - Market breadth (advance/decline)
   - Major stock movers

2. **Crypto Markets**
   - Bitcoin and Ethereum prices
   - Altcoin trends
   - ETF inflows/outflows
   - Whale activity

3. **Macro Drivers**
   - Fed policy and rate decisions
   - Economic data releases
   - Bond yields and interest rates
   - Geopolitical events

4. **Market Sentiment**
   - VIX and volatility metrics
   - Market breadth divergences
   - Institutional fund flows
   - Options positioning

**Synthesis Focus:** What moved markets today and why

---

## Hidden Report (`hidden`)

**Purpose:** Discover unusual opportunities and structural risks missed by mainstream

**Research Angles (6 parallel):**

1. **Penny Stocks**
   - Stocks up 50%+ in day/week
   - Unusual volume spikes
   - Meme stock rallies

2. **Whale Positioning**
   - Crypto whale positions
   - Large short/long builds
   - Smart money vs retail divergence

3. **Market Structure**
   - Breadth divergences
   - Leadership concentration
   - Small-cap vs large-cap gaps

4. **Emerging Markets**
   - Currency depreciation
   - Capital flight patterns
   - Contagion risks

5. **Altcoins/DeFi**
   - Tokens gaining 20%+ while BTC flat
   - New exchange listings
   - Whale accumulation

6. **Hidden Catalysts**
   - Under-reported regulatory news
   - Technical breakdowns
   - Index rebalancing effects

**Synthesis Focus:** Opportunities + risks with actionable thresholds

---

## Sector Report (`sector`)

**Purpose:** Deep dive into sector rotation patterns

**Research Angles (4 parallel):**

1. **Sector Performance**
   - 11-sector performance ranking
   - Momentum leaders/laggards

2. **Rotation Patterns**
   - Defensive vs cyclical shifts
   - Historical rotation timing

3. **Factor Analysis**
   - Size, value, growth tilts
   - Quality and momentum factors

4. **Sector Catalysts**
   - Regulatory impacts by sector
   - Earnings trends by sector

**Synthesis Focus:** Identify sector rotation opportunity

---

## Technical Report (`technical`)

**Purpose:** Analyze technical setups and price action

**Research Angles (4 parallel):**

1. **Index Technicals**
   - Support/resistance levels
   - Moving average status
   - Trend strength

2. **Breakouts & Breakdowns**
   - New highs/lows
   - Chart pattern formations

3. **Volume & Momentum**
   - Volume profile analysis
   - Momentum indicator divergences

4. **Technical Risk Levels**
   - Key threshold levels
   - Invalidation points
   - Risk/reward setups

**Synthesis Focus:** Actionable technical entry/exit levels

---

## Quality Standards (All Types)

- **100% Citation Coverage** - Every claim sourced
- **Footnote Format** - `[^1]`, `[^2]` with references section
- **12+ Sources** per research angle minimum
- **Parallel Execution** - All research angles run simultaneously
- **Professional Structure** - Clear sections, logical flow
