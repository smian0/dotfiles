# Stock Market Movement Research - November 10, 2025

## Executive Summary

U.S. stock markets posted significant gains on November 10, 2025, with all three major indices closing higher. The rally was primarily driven by Senate progress on government shutdown negotiations and strong tech sector performance.

## Market Performance

**Dow Jones Industrial Average**
- Closed at 47,368.63
- Gained 380 points (+0.81%)
- Source: [CNBC Markets](https://www.cnbc.com/markets/)

**S&P 500**
- Closed at 6,832.43
- Gained 103.63 points (+1.54%)
- Source: [CNBC Markets](https://www.cnbc.com/markets/)

**Nasdaq Composite**
- Closed at 23,527.17
- Gained 522.64 points (+2.27%)
- Strongest performer among the three major indices
- Source: [CNBC Markets](https://www.cnbc.com/markets/)

**Government Shutdown Negotiations**

- Positive sentiment around political stability supported market gains
- Source: [MarketWatch](https://www.marketwatch.com/)

**Technology Sector Strength**
- Tech stocks led the rally, driving Nasdaq's outperformance
- Broad-based buying across growth stocks
- Source: [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/)
